FAQS

When do you deliver?

Our main delivery hours are between midnight and 6 am but if needed other arrangements can be made.

Any time after 10 pm someone will come by to pick the animal up.

Should I pre-book an animal?

Yes, even though we have several of each animal available they do book fast. We recommend that you book 2 weeks in advance to ensure the prop of your choice.

Can I rent an animal last minute?

Sure, give us a call we will let you know if there is anything available. It doesn't hurt to try!
